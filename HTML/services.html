Our Services & Capabilities

Empowering your business with innovative solutions and expert support.

Custom Manufacturing Services

Providing engineered manufacturing solutions tailored to meet unique fabrication requirements. Utilizing advanced design methodologies and precision fabrication technologies—including CNC machining, plasma cutting, and multi-process welding—we deliver custom-built components and assemblies optimized for performance, durability, and application-specific functionality.

Welding Services

Offering a comprehensive range of precision welding solutions for both custom fabrication and repair applications. Available welding processes include MIG (GMAW), TIG (GTAW), Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW), and Spot Welding, among others. We specialize in working with a wide variety of materials, including but not limited to carbon steel, stainless steel, aluminum, cast iron, brass (brazing), and select thermoplastics. Services encompass structural fabrication, component repair, prototyping, and modification to meet exact specifications and industry standards.

Parts Manufacturing

Equipped with a high-precision CNC Plasma Arc Cutting Table capable of producing complex, repeatable components with tight tolerances. Suitable for both large-scale and small-scale part fabrication, this system ensures consistent accuracy and efficiency in high-volume or custom production runs.
